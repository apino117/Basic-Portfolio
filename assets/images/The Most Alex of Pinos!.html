About Me

Hi there! My name is Alex, I’m an artist. I grew up in the suburbs of DC, childhood on the Maryland side, teenage years in Virginia. My area in Maryland was heavily wooded so from an early age I found myself in love with nature and with connecting to the earth. As I got older I realized I was channeling that same love into my art, I’ve always had a hand for the visual arts but my real affinity lies with music. To quote Albus Dumbledore music is “a magic beyond all we do here” and although the “here” to which he was referring is the magical school Hogwarts I believe we can apply it to our existence as humans. What I mean when I say that is that music has ways of affecting us that we don’t even fully comprehend, ways we don’t even notice. If you’ve ever had someone point out a particular chord change or flourish and note the emotional attachment you may be familiar with the “ah-ha” moment of putting words to a feeling you’ve never realized you were experiencing.

The reason I mention music is that it’s a very convenient metaphor for the framework with which I view life and communication. Just like a minor chord elicits a sad emotion there are variables in our day-to-day lives of which we may not be explicitly aware but which have a profound affect on our perception and therefore our emotions. My goal therefore as a developer is to bring an artistic and philosophical ethos into the context of web design and creation. In design every detail from the small to the large has a crucial role to play when manipulating the effect your product will have. Ultimately this applies to everything in life, from the color of stop signs to the width of a sidewalk; every single detail is relevant and chains together. When you hire me as a developer you’re getting someone who thinks beyond the surface level, deeper than what’s necessary; what’s affective. There is no opportunity too small for an artistic approach and there is no product without room for analysis. My ongoing work is to continue to note analyze and implement artistic values in every aspect of my life because ultimately I believe this will lead to a better quality of life not only for myself but for the whole world.
